Project Overview
This project will consist of designing and developing a multi-page website that promotes and
supports an online course focused on social media management for small business owners.
The website will introduce users to the course, explain the value of learning social media
marketing, and provide structured information on how business owners can take control of their
online presence.
Purpose of the Project
The purpose of this website is to simplify social media marketing for small business owners who
may feel overwhelmed or unable to afford professional services.
This course is built on the belief that anyone can successfully manage their own social media
with the right guidance, structure, and tools. The website will act as both a promotional platform
and an educational resource.
